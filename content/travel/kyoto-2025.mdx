---
title: "The Bamboo Forests"
date: "04.12.2025 - 04.18.2025"
location: "Kyoto, Japan"
summary: "Tracing the ancient paths through Arashiyama. A study in natural architecture."
---

The visual transition from Tokyo's neon to Kyoto's organic geometry is jarring in the best way possible. The bamboo stalks create a natural sorting algorithm, filtering the sunlight into parallel vertical lines. 

I spent most of the morning mapping out the visual density of the forest, noting how the sound of the wind completely changes when passing through the hollow wood versus the concrete canyons of the city.
